The number of questions per section as per the given combination could vary with mocks as well as the ongoing mock.

Tie breaker guidelines (Posted on 10-SEP-2023)

The following tie breaker guidelines have been suggested:

  1. The initial subject to tie breaker is the score achieved by the candidate in quantitative reasoning
  2. The next subject to tie breaker is the score achieved by the candidate in verbal reasoning
  3. If the scores are still the same, then the scores achieved by the candidate in the AWA section would be considered
  4. If for some reason the scores still match, then the date on which the candidate registered would be considered as tie breaker. The candidate who would have registered earlier would stand out in terms of the same
  5. If the tie breaker still exists, the same position would be given to all the candidates involved.

**This tie breaker exists in case there are multiple candidates for one seat.
