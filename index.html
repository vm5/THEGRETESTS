About the GRE

The Graduate Record Examinations is a standardized test that is an admissions requirement for many graduate schools in the United States and Canada, as well as a few other countries. The GRE is owned and administered by Educational Testing Service.

One Test. More Opportunities for Success.

Whether you are planning to go to graduate, business, or law school or just exploring your options, you are taking an important step toward your future. It is a smart move to show schools your best with the GRE General Test.

We are here to support you with resources that help you prepare for the test, connect with recruiters, build a competitive application and more.
